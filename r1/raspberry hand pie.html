Raspberry hand pie

These raspberry hand pies, also known as turnovers, are the perfect combination of sweetness of the raspberry filling and saltiness of the flaky crust. Ideal for picnic or as an afternoon treat.

Directions!

These hand pies are made from two components: the pie crust and the raspberry filling. Of course you can use a pre-made pie crust if you like, just skip steps 1, 2, 3 and jump right into making the raspberry filling.

  1. Prep crust:

    Start by whisking all the dry ingredients for the crust in a large mixing bowl. this will be fluor, sugar and salt.

    Next you want to cut the cold butter into small pieces and add it to the mixing bowl with the dry ingredients. It's very important that the butter is extremely cold because this will help you make the crust very flaky.
