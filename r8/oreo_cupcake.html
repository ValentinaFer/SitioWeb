Oreo Cupcake!

Chocolate cupcake with a hidden oreo at the bottom and topped with creamy oreo frosting!

  • Ingredients
  • Directions
  • Opinions

Ingredients!

Check the boxes if you have the ingredients

Servings: 16

Directions!

Make sure to whip air into the cake during creaming process since this cake doesn't have any leaving. It'll provide the lift in the pound cake you want!

Cupcakes:

  1. Preheat oven to 170 degrees C and line the muffin tray with paper muffin cases. twist 12 oreos to separate them and place half in each case, with the cream frosting facing upwards.

  2. Put the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, salt and butter in a large bowl and mix until you get a sandy consistency and everything is combined.

  3. Whisk the milk, eggs, and vanilla extract together in a separate jug, then slowly add half into the flour mixture, and beat well to get rid of any lumps. Gradually add the rest of the milk mixture whilst mixing until smooth.

  4. Add the mixture to the paper cases, making sure they are equally full (about two-thirds). Bake the cakes in the oven for 20-25 minutes.

Frosting:

  1. Whilst the cakes are cooking, smash up 6 Oreos in a bag and set aside. Whisk the butter, cream cheese and icing sugar until light and fluffy. Then, gently fold in the Oreo Crumbs.

  2. When the cakes have cooled, pipe the icing onto the cakes and use any left over Oreos to decorate.
