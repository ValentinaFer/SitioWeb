Fruit Galette!

Galettes are rustic little-form pies that can be easily and quickly assembled and then you can eat them warm out of the oven! simple, humble and beautiful. Also, you can easily subtitute pears or peaches for the apples or even add other fruits such as blueberries or raspberries.

  • Ingredients
  • Directions
  • Opinions

Ingredients!

Check the boxes if you have the ingredients

Servings: 4

Directions!

If you want extra sparkle, brush the crust with a glaze of 1 egg white, 1 tablespoon water, and the sprinkle a teaspoon sugar over the crust and fruit.

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease a baking sheet

  2. Combine apples, sugar, cornstarch, cinnamon and vanilla extract together in a bowl.

    Place the pie crust on the prepared baking sheet. Spoon apple mixture into the center of the crust, leaving a 2-inch border. Fold crust over the edges of the apple mixture.

  3. Bake in the preheated oven until apples are tender and crust is golden, about 30 minutes.
